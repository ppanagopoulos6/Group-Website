INDIA

About India

India is a vast and diverse country full of history, culture, and natural beauty. From the majestic Himalayas in the north to tropical beaches in the south, India offers something for every traveler. Visitors can explore ancient temples, vibrant cities, colorful markets, and UNESCO World Heritage sites like the Taj Mahal.

Famous Cities

Delhi

Delhi is India’s capital and a city of contrasts, where ancient history meets modern life. Tourists can explore landmarks like the Red Fort, Qutub Minar, and India Gate.

Jaipur

Jaipur is the capital of Rajasthan and part of the famous Golden Triangle. Known as the “Pink City,” it attracts tourists with its grand palaces and forts.

Agra

Agra is best known as the home of the world-famous Taj Mahal, one of the Seven Wonders of the World. Visitors also enjoy Agra Fort and the city’s rich Mughal history.

Mumbai

Mumbai is India’s largest city and the heart of its film industry, Bollywood. Tourists come to see landmarks like the Gateway of India and relax along Marine Drive.

The Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal is an ivory-white marble mausoleum commissioned in 1631 by the emperor Shah Jahan. It is regarded as one of the best examples of Mughal architecture.

Must Taste

  • Butter Chicken: A rich and creamy tomato-based chicken curry, perfect with naan or rice.
  • Biryani: A fragrant spiced rice dish cooked with meat or vegetables, often served with yogurt.
  • Samosa: Deep-fried pastry pockets filled with spiced potatoes, peas, or meat.
  • Masala Dosa: A crispy rice and lentil crepe filled with spiced potato.
  • Paneer Tikka: Marinated cubes of Indian cottage cheese grilled to perfection.

Tips for Travelers

  • Carry cash and small change – Many markets and small vendors don’t take cards.
  • Dress modestly – Cover shoulders and knees, especially at temples.
  • Stay hydrated – Drink bottled water and choose clean restaurants.
  • Use reliable transport – Prepaid taxis and ride-hailing apps are safest.
