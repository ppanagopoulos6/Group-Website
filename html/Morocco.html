MOROCCO

About Morocco

Morocco is a country where history, culture, and landscape come together. Located at the crossroads of Africa, Europe, and the Arab world, Morocco has been shaped by Berber civilizations, Arab dynasties, and Mediterranean influences for thousands of years.

Top Destinations

1. Marrakech

Marrakech is known for its intense colors and lively atmosphere. The heart of the city is Jemaa el-Fnaa, a huge square filled with performers and markets.

2. Fez

One of the oldest cities in Morocco, famous for its medieval old town (Fez el-Bali) and traditional leather tanneries.

3. Essaouira

A charming port town on the Atlantic Ocean with historic walls, a fishing harbor with blue boats, and a relaxed atmosphere.

4. Casablanca

Morocco’s largest city, home to the Hassan II Mosque, one of the largest and most beautiful in the country.

5. Chefchaouen

Famous for its dreamy blue-painted old town. Walking through its narrow alleys feels almost magical.

Moroccan Cuisine

Tagine

A slow-cooked stew made with meat, vegetables, and spices, cooked in a traditional clay pot.

Couscous

Steamed semolina grains served with vegetables and often meat — a classic Moroccan meal.

Harira

A traditional soup made with lentils, chickpeas, tomatoes, and spices, especially popular during Ramadan.
