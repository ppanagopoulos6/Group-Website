CHINA

About China

China has one of the world’s oldest continuous histories, dating back over 5,000 years. It offers everything from modern megacities to ancient temples, deserts, mountains, and rivers.

Cities You Must Visit

  • Beijing: China’s capital, home to the Great Wall and Forbidden City.
  • Shanghai: A vibrant global city with a futuristic skyline.
  • Xi’an: Famous for the Terracotta Army and ancient city walls.

Historic Sites

The Forbidden City

The Forbidden City is the largest preserved royal palace complex in the world, located in the center of Beijing.

The Great Wall of China

The Great Wall is a series of fortifications built to protect the ancient Chinese states. It is one of the New 7 Wonders of the World.

Terracotta Army

The Terracotta Army is a collection of sculptures depicting the armies of the first emperor of China.

Potala Palace

Potala Palace in Lhasa is a historic museum complex that served as the residence of the Dalai Lamas.

Must Taste

  • Peking Duck: Crispy roasted duck, famous in Beijing.
  • Xiaolongbao: Soup dumplings filled with meat and broth.
  • Chow Mein: Stir-fried noodles with vegetables and meat.
  • Chinese Tea: A big part of Chinese culture.

Tips

  • Get your visa early.
  • Mobile payments are widely used.
  • Respect local customs and be polite.
  • Be prepared for internet restrictions.
