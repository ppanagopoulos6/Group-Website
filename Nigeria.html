NIGERIA

About Nigeria

Nigeria is a large and vibrant country located in West Africa and is often called the “Giant of Africa.” It is known for its rich culture, music, films, and diverse traditions.

Top Destinations

1. Lagos

Lagos is the largest city in Nigeria. It is known for its beaches, nightlife, and business centers like Victoria Island.

2. Abuja

Abuja is Nigeria’s planned capital. It is a modern, orderly city home to important government buildings and parks.

3. Zuma Rock

Zuma Rock is a huge natural rock formation near Abuja. It is over 725 meters tall and looks like a giant face.

4. Yankari National Park

One of Nigeria’s most important wildlife reserves, home to elephants, baboons, and natural warm springs.

5. Olumo Rock

A historic site in Abeokuta used as a fortress during tribal wars. It offers panoramic views of the city.

Nigerian Cuisine

Jollof Rice

A popular dish made with rice, tomatoes, onions, and spices, often served at parties.

Egusi Soup

A thick soup made from ground melon seeds, vegetables, and meat, eaten with pounded yam or fufu.

Pounded Yam

A traditional staple made by pounding boiled yam until smooth and stretchy.
